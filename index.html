▸ HOW TO PLAY STOXELS

WHAT IS A NONOGRAM?

A nonogram is a grid puzzle. Numbers along rows and columns tell you how many consecutive filled cells exist. Reveal the hidden symbol to win!

CONTROLS

Left click — fill a cell (wrong fills cost time!)
Right click — mark a cell with ✕ (safe, no penalty)
Drag — paint multiple cells
Escape — go back to previous screen

DIFFICULTY

Easy — small penalties (×1), ×0.5 score
Normal — standard (×2), ×1 score
Hard — heavy penalties (×4), ×1.5 score

MODIFIERS

Time Trial — only 5 minutes!
Hardcore — one wrong tile = instant fail
Ironman — no items allowed, +15% score

ITEMS & INVENTORY

Complete the level's bonus objective to earn ONE random item. Items range from Common to Legendary — rarer items are more powerful. Corrupted items are gambles! Items cost score to use, and can be sold. Ironman disables all item use.

BONUS OBJECTIVE & QUIZ

Each level has a bonus objective (shown on the level card). If you meet the bonus objective, a stats multiple-choice quiz appears after a brief pause — answer correctly for +50 pts and a bonus item! If you miss the objective, no quiz appears.

MOODLE CODES

Complete all levels in a World to unlock a Moodle Code. Enter it in Moodle to earn a badge that is stored in your Moodle profile.
